Who are We?

We are the Linux Users' Group of Durgapur.The group started in 2004. A few small brains grouped together to populate Linux in the locality. Our motto is শেখ এবং শেখাও (Sekho ebang sekhao) which is a bengali phrase meaning learn and teach others. We help out people to learn technologies with primary focus to guide them in contributing to upstream and nuture new open source contributors.

Venue

The National Institute of Technology, Durgapur also known as NIT Durgapur or NITDGP, is a public engineering college located in Durgapur, West Bengal, India. The institute is the home to a lot of open source contributors contributing to various organization like GNOME, KDE, Mozilla, Fedora etc.
